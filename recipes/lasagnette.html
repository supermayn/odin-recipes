Lasagnette

Recepie for a juicy and delicious lasagnette with bechamelsauce. Lasagnette is always a hit among adults and children.

This recipe is designed for food lovers in a hurry!

  • Total cook time: 60 min
  • Active time: 30 min
  • Servings: 4-6

Ingredients:

  • 1 red onion, minced
  • 500 g ground beef
  • 2-3 carrots, grated
  • 2 cans of chopped tomatoes
  • 3 cloves of garlic, pressed
  • 100 g tomato pasta
  • 2 dl milk
  • 1 beef boullion cube in 2 dl water
  • 2 tsp oregano
  • 400 g pasta screws
  • 150g cheese of your preference

bechamelsauce:

  1. 1,5 tbsp butter
  2. 1,5 tbsp flour
  3. 3 dl milk
  4. 1 tsp nutmeg

Directions:

  1. Saute ground beef in a big pan.
  2. Add red onion and garlic and let it cook together for a minute or two.
  3. Add carrots, chopped tomatoes, tomato pasta, milk and 2 dl beef boullion.
  4. Stir and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Make bechamelsauce by melting butter in a pot. Add flour and slowly bit by bit add milk.
  6. Let bechamelsauce cook for a few minutes and taste it by adding nutmeg, salt and pepper. For extra taste add grated cheese.
  7. Distribute pasta screws in an ovenproof dish and pour meat sauce over it.
  8. Lastly pour bechamelsauce over it and sprinkle with cheese.
