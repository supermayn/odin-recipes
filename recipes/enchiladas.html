Enchiladas

It is always satisfing and delicious when enchiladas meet your mouth! This recipe is filled with vegetables, meat and overall great taste.

Easy, tasty and fast to make.

  • Cook time: 40 min
  • Active time: 25 min
  • Servings: 4-6

Ingredients:

  • 1 onion, minced
  • 500 g ground beef
  • 2 carrots, grated
  • 200 g squash, grated
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, pressed
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground oregano
  • 0,5 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 can of chopped tomatoes
  • 1 can of beans
  • 150 g corn, frozen
  • 8 tortilla pancakes
  • 150 g cheddar
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Directions:

  1. Saute onion in a pot with oil.
  2. Add ground beef and cook in high temperature while stirring it.
  3. Add carrots, squash, bell peppers, garlic, spices and tomatoes.
  4. Stir and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Add beans and corn and cook for another 5 minutes.
  6. Pour the meat sauce into the wraps. Roll them up together and lay down in ovenproof dish.
  7. Sprinkle cheddar cheese over all of enchiladas.
  8. Lastly sprinkle oregano onto cheese.
